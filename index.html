Welcome to OSIMLAI Adventures Ltd

Our adventure, nature, culture and beach holiday tours are designed to share the unique cultural and environmental marvels of Tanzania whilst positively impacting our local communities and conserving our natural landscapes.

At OSIMLAI Adventure, our impact is to stimulate growth within our beautiful community in Engaruka village. By booking with our company. Your are directly investing in the development of our MASAI community.

Feel at Home

Our Offers

The Company is a native-run that is based in Arusha, the gateway to Tanzania's glorious national Parks. We specialize in authentic , sustainable tours throughout Tanzania and offer a variety of packages that will take you off the beaten track.

Also, the Company was launched to share enthusiasm of Maasai village of Engaruka which is located north of Tanzania on the floor of the great East African Rift Valley, is where the CEO of Company was born and raised.

Economic Growth

Whilst reinvesting in our environment and youth drives economic growth, we also aim to encourage women to pursue careers.

Environmental care

We introduce courses at local schools that teach pupils to safeguard water, repurpose waste and protect the fauna and flora that characterise our beautiful surroundings.

Youth Support

We believe that education is a powerful tool to overcome poverty and the youth of Engaruka are our future. Children that do not have access to an education are left vulnerable to child labour exploitation and immense poverty.

Profit Sharing

25% of all booking profits and 100% of all donations are directly invested in our Masai community.

Sponsorship

We sponsor struggling children to ensure that they attend and complete school successfully. We also partner with local schools to provide school materials, food supplies and other resources.

Workshops

We hold monthly workshops with the village’s Women's Group that focuses on improving entrepreneurial skills, turning business ideas into reality and economic opportunities that can help support their immediate families.

The Company's C.E.O Dream

To give back to her community pass on her parents desire to open doors to further female education in the Maasai community. With experience, expertise and passion for the tourism industry, the Company C.E.O is devoted to providing a unique, tantalising experience to the clients.

Our Routes

We normally use the following tour routes with our clients directed by our experienced Tour guides so as to make sure the rate of enjoyment and thirsty satisfaction is well undertaken.

Marangu Route

Duration: 6 Days

The Marangu route, also known as the “Coca-Cola” route, is the oldest, most well established route on Kilimanjaro.

Shira Route Kilimanjaro

Duration: 6 Days

Does Kilimanjaro hold a special fascination for you? Are you planning to realise your dream through the Shira Route?.

Rongai Route Kilimanjaro

Duration: 7 Days

The Rongai route is the only route that approaches Kilimanjaro from the north, close to the Kenyan border.

Umbwe Route Kilimanjaro

Duration: 7 Days

The Umbwe route has a well-deserved reputation of being the most challenging route on Mount Kilimanjaro.

Lake Manyara Route

Duration: 5 Days

This is an 5 Days safari taking you to the bush for 5 days with safaris starting from Arusha.

Midrange Safari Route

Duration: 2 Days & 3 Days

The Serengeti National Park is famous for good reason. It is the scene of the incredible annual migration of 1.5 million wildebeest.

Machame Route Kilimanjaro

Duration: 7 Days

The Machame route, also known as the “Whiskey” route, is the most popular route on Kilimanjaro. Machame’s draw is in its scenic beauty.

Lemosho Route Kilimanjaro

Duration: 9 Days

The Lemosho route is one of the newer routes on Mount Kilimanjaro. The route begins in the west and rather than simply intersecting Shira Plateau (like Machame).

Serengeti Wildebeest Migration Safari Route

Duration: 9 Days
